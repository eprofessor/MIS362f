Business Intelligence and Analytics



700 Points
Learning Objectives

These exercises introduce concepts used to define how organizations use business intelligence and analytics in information systems.

After completing this module you will be able to:

  1. Define the terms business intelligence (BI) and analytics
  2.  Provide several real-world examples of BI and analytics being used to improve decision making
  3.  Identify the key components that must be in place for an organization to get real value from its BI and analytics efforts
  4.  Identify several BI techniques and discuss how they are used
  5.  Identify several BI tools
  6.  Define the term self-service analytics and discuss its pros and cons
