Knowledge Management and Specialized Information Systems



800 Points
Learning Objectives

These exercises introduce concepts used to define how organizations use knowledge management and specialized information systems.

After completing this module you will be able to:

  1. Discuss the relationships between data, information, and knowledge
  2.  Identify the benefits associated with a sound knowledge management program
  3.  List some of the tools and techniques used in knowledge management
  4.  Define the term 'artificial intelligence' and state the objective of developing artificial intelligence systems
  5.  List the characteristics of intelligent behavior and compare the performance of natural and artificial intelligence systems for each of these characteristics
  6.  Identify the major components of the artificial intelligence field and provide one example of each type of system
  7.  Discuss the use of multimedia in a business setting
  8.  Define the terms 'virtual reality' and 'augmented reality' and provide examples of these applications
  9.  Discuss examples of specialized systems for organizational and individual use.
