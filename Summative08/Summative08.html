Enterprise Systems



450 Points
Learning Objectives

These exercises introduce concepts used to define how organizations use enterprise to meet their objectives.

After completing this module you will be able to:

  1. Identify the basic activities and business objectives common to all transaction processing systems
  2.  Describe the transaction processing systems associated with the order processing, purchasing, and accounting business functions
  3.  Identify the basic functions performed and the benefits derived from the implementation of an enterprise resource planning system, customer resource management, and product lifecycle management system
  4.  Describe the hosted software model for enterprise systems and explain why this approach is so appealing to SMEs
  5.  Identify the challenges that organizations face in planning, building, and operating their enterprise systems
  6.  Identify tips for avoiding many of the common causes for failed enterprise system implementations
